## Core Concepts

### The Four Ways of Knowing (4Ps)

**Propositional** - Knowing THAT (facts, information)
- Information content, statistical patterns
- See: [RR Overview](concepts/00-relevance-realization/00-overview.md#four-ways-of-knowing)
- Application: [knowing.py](ARR-COC-VIS-Application-Guide.md#propositional-knowing) in ARR-COC-VIS

**Procedural** - Knowing HOW (skills, automaticity)
- Learned compression strategies
- See: [RR Overview](concepts/00-relevance-realization/00-overview.md#four-ways-of-knowing)
- Application: Quality adapter in ARR-COC-VIS

**Perspectival** - Knowing WHAT IT'S LIKE (salience landscapes)
- Multi-scale processing, perceptual organization
- See: [RR Overview](concepts/00-relevance-realization/00-overview.md#four-ways-of-knowing)
- Application: [balancing.py](ARR-COC-VIS-Application-Guide.md#perspectival-knowing) in ARR-COC-VIS

**Participatory** - Knowing BY BEING (agent-arena coupling)
- Query-content coupling, transjective relevance
- See: [Transjective Overview](concepts/01-transjective/00-overview.md)
- Application: Cross-attention scoring in ARR-COC-VIS

### Opponent Processing

Three dimensions of cognitive tension navigation:

**1. Cognitive Scope: Compression ↔ Particularization**
- See: [Vervaeke 2012](papers/00-Vervaeke-2012-Primary-Paper-Analysis.md#opponent-processing)
- Implementation: [ARR-COC-VIS](ARR-COC-VIS-Application-Guide.md#compression-particularization) - 64-400 tokens per patch

**2. Cognitive Tempering: Exploit ↔ Explore**
- See: [Kaaij 2022](papers/01-Kaaij-2022-Frame-Problem-Analysis.md#opponent-dimensions)
- Status: NOT implemented in ARR-COC-VIS (enhancement opportunity)

**3. Cognitive Prioritization: Focus ↔ Diversify**
- See: [Kaaij 2022](papers/01-Kaaij-2022-Frame-Problem-Analysis.md#opponent-dimensions)
- Status: NOT implemented in ARR-COC-VIS (enhancement opportunity)

## Key Distinctions

### Attention vs Relevance Realization
- **Attention**: Fixed QKV mechanism, uniform resolution, mechanistic
- **Relevance Realization**: Dynamic process, variable LOD, opponent processing
- See: [Vision Materials](papers/05-Vision-Perception-Attention-Materials.md)

### Salience vs Relevance
- **Salience**: What captures attention (bottom-up)
- **Relevance**: What is actually important (transjective)
- See: [RR Overview](concepts/00-relevance-realization/00-overview.md)

### Objective vs Transjective
- **Objective**: Property of stimulus alone
- **Subjective**: Property of mind alone
- **Transjective**: Emerges from agent-arena coupling
- See: [Transjective Overview](concepts/01-transjective/00-overview.md)

### Large World vs Small World
- **Large World**: Ill-defined problems, continuous novelty (biology)
- **Small World**: Well-defined problems, finite states (computation)
- See: [Jaeger 2024](papers/03-Jaeger-2024-Naturalizing-RR-Analysis.md)

### Intelligence vs Wisdom
- **Intelligence**: Information processing capability (AI can achieve)
- **Rationality**: Overcoming self-deception (AI may approximate)
- **Wisdom**: Caring for reality beyond self (AI cannot achieve)
- **Consciousness**: Phenomenal experience (AI cannot achieve)
- See: [Academic Dialogue](concepts/02-academic-dialogue/00-overview.md)
