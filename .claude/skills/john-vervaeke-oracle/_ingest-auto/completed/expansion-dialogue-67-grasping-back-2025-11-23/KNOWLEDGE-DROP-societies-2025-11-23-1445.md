## Summary

The file explores how the self emerges as a "society of occasions" displaying a "unity of style" - a pattern of value realization that persists through constant arising and perishing.

## Key Concepts Added

### Core Whiteheadian Concepts
- **Societies**: Nexus with social order through mutual prehension
- **Enduring Objects**: Purely temporal societies (single strand)
- **Corpuscular Societies**: Multiple strands (rocks, tables)
- **Living Societies**: Where novelty emerges
- **Satisfaction**: Completion of an occasion's becoming
- **Perishing**: Transition from subjective immediacy to objective immortality
- **Objective Immortality**: Eternal relevance as datum for future
- **Unity of Style**: Defining characteristic persisting across occasions
- **Art of Life**: Threefold urge to live, live well, live better

### Personal Identity Account
- Self as "personally ordered society"
- Three senses of self: occasion, historic route, defining characteristic
- Identity as achievement not given
- Neither substance nor mere bundle
