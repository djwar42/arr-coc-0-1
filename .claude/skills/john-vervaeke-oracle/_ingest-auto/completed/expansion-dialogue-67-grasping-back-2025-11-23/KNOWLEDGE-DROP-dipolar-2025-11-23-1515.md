## Key Concepts Covered

### The Dipolar Structure
- Physical pole: Reception of past actual world through physical prehensions
- Mental pole: Entertainment of possibilities through conceptual prehensions
- Two phases: Conceptual reproduction and conceptual reversion
- Subjective aim: Guiding integration toward satisfaction

### Creativity
- "The many become one, and are increased by one"
- Creativity as ultimate metaphysical principle
- Novel synthesis transcends mere rearrangement
- Genuine becoming, not predetermined unfolding

## Vervaekean Coloring Applied

### 4Ps Mapping
- **Propositional**: Emerges from integration of facts (physical) and logical relations (mental)
- **Procedural**: Skills as inherited patterns open to improvement
- **Perspectival**: Salience determined by dipolar integration under subjective aim
- **Participatory**: Identity as ongoing dipolar synthesis

### Opponent Processing: Determination vs. Freedom
- Physical pole = Determination (what must be taken into account)
- Mental pole = Freedom (what might be realized)
- Neither alone sufficient - integration produces constrained creativity
- Additional processes: Conformity/Transformation, Reception/Anticipation, Actuality/Possibility

### Meaning Crisis Connection
- Balancing tradition (physical) and innovation (mental)
- Recovery of temporal coherence
- Wisdom as optimal dipolar integration
- Subjective aim as model for purposive existence

### RR Integration (15%)
- **Relevance realization IS dipolar integration**
- Salience landscape = synthesis of physical data and mental possibilities under subjective aim
- Agent-arena relationship has dipolar structure (transjective)
- Opponent processing in RR maps to dipolar balance (exploitation/exploration, compression/particularization)
- Concrescence = RR process
