## Summary

## Key Concepts Covered

### Core Jamesian Insights
- **Stream not chain**: Consciousness flows continuously, not as linked discrete ideas
- **Substantive/Transitive parts**: Flights and perchings - the rhythm of thought
- **The Fringe**: Psychic overtone, halo, suffusion - felt relations around every content
- **Feelings of relation**: "A feeling of *and*, a feeling of *if*, a feeling of *but*"
- **Selective attention**: Experience constituted by interested selection
- **Tip-of-tongue phenomenon**: The gap that actively beckons

### Vervaekean Coloring Applied

**4Ps Mapping**:
- Propositional: Fringe is pre-propositional; feelings exceed propositions
- Procedural: Transitive states are the "how" of cognition; skilled anticipation
- Perspectival: Fringe as salience landscape; topic-relative relevance
- Participatory: Personal ownership; warmth of self; caring drives selection

**Opponent Processing**:
