## Key Theoretical Connections

### 1. Concrescence = Relevance Realization

Both describe selective synthesis of multiplicity into meaningful unity:
- **Whitehead**: "The many become one and are increased by one"
- **Vervaeke**: From infinite possibilities, dynamically determine what matters

The process is fundamentally **non-computational**--aesthetic rather than algorithmic.

### 2. Prehension = Perspectival Knowing

Whitehead's "grasping without comprehension" IS the mechanism of salience:
- Subjective form of prehensions determines what stands out
- Positive prehensions = realized relevance
- Negative prehensions = background/context creation
- Pre-propositional knowing in both frameworks

### 3. Process = Becoming Relevant

Ontological reversal: things don't exist then become relevant; they BECOME through relevance realization.
