### 2. How Autopoiesis Grounds RR

From Thompson (2004): "Living is cognitive, and cognizing is living."

**Key Points**:

### 3. Life-Mind Continuity

From MDPI (Wiese 2021): "There is a strong continuity between life and mind, and there are good reasons for assuming a continuity between autopoiesis and cognition."

**Implications for RR**:

**Complete Mapping**:

- Procedural = Knowing how to engage
- Propositional = Articulating what is (derivative)

### 5. Opponent Processing = Enactivist Tensions

**Key Tensions**:
- Autonomy <-> Heteronomy
- Individual <-> Social
- Conservation <-> Innovation
- Retention <-> Protention

These tensions ARE opponent processing - navigating them produces adaptive flexibility.

### 6. Meaning Crisis as Sense-Making Breakdown

**Enactivist Diagnosis**:

- Loss of embodied, participatory engagement
- Breakdown at individual, social, and cultural levels
