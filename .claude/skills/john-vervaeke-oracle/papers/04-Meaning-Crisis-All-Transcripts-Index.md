## What is the Awakening from the Meaning Crisis Series

This page presents what the meaning crisis is in the view of John Vervaeke. The series consists of 50 comprehensive episodes exploring the historical, philosophical, and cognitive-scientific dimensions of the meaning crisis and potential responses to it.

## Key Themes Throughout Series

### Historical Arc
1. **Axial Revolution** (Episodes 2-8): Socrates, Plato, Aristotle, Buddha
2. **Hellenistic & Christian Era** (Episodes 14-20): Stoics, Jesus, Gnosticism, Neoplatonism
3. **Medieval to Modern** (Episodes 20-25): Scientific Revolution, Descartes, Romanticism, Hegel
4. **20th Century Crisis** (Episodes 25-26): Wars, philosophy, birth of Cognitive Science

- **Episodes 26-32**: Relevance Realization theory development
- **Episodes 33-35**: Spirituality, sacredness, symbols
- **Episodes 36-39**: Reverse engineering enlightenment

- **Episodes 40-45**: Deep dive into wisdom, rationality, and their relationship

### Philosophical Integration
