## Key Insights Summary

### Core Framework
**Relevance Realization** is the cognitive process that solves the frame problem - determining what matters from infinite possibilities through opponent processing without combinatorial explosion.

### Three Opponent Dimensions
1. **Cognitive Scope**: Compression ‚Üî Particularization
2. **Cognitive Tempering**: Exploit ‚Üî Explore
3. **Cognitive Prioritization**: Focus ‚Üî Diversify

### Four Ways of Knowing (4Ps)
1. **Propositional** - Knowing THAT (facts, information)
2. **Procedural** - Knowing HOW (skills, automaticity)
3. **Perspectival** - Knowing WHAT IT'S LIKE (salience landscapes)
4. **Participatory** - Knowing BY BEING (agent-arena coupling)

### Transjective Relevance
Neither objective (in world) nor subjective (in mind), but emerges from agent-arena coupling. Like a shark's fitness for the ocean.

### Large World vs Small World
- **Large World**: Ill-defined problems, ambiguous info, continuous novelty (biology)
- **Small World**: Well-defined problems, clear ontologies, finite states (computation)

### ARR-COC-VIS Status
- **Implements**: Cognitive scope (compression-particularization), query-aware relevance
- **Missing**: Cognitive tempering (exploit-explore), cognitive prioritization (focus-diversify)
- **Score**: 13/25 on Kaaij's RR capability scale (same as CLARION)
- **Position**: Between standard CV and biological RR - "RR-inspired" not "RR-implementing"

### Vervaeke on AI
**Skeptical About**: AI consciousness, wisdom, true RR (fundamentally non-computational)
**Hopeful About**: AI as cognitive tool, wisdom-conducive systems, human-AI complementarity

## Critical Distinctions

### Attention vs Relevance Realization
- **Attention**: Fixed QKV mechanism, uniform resolution, mechanistic
- **RR**: Dynamic process, variable LOD, opponent processing, realized not computed

### Salience vs Relevance
- **Salience**: What captures attention (bottom-up)
- **Relevance**: What is actually important (transjective, realized through process)

### Objective vs Transjective
- **Objective**: Property of stimulus alone
- **Subjective**: Property of mind alone
- **Transjective**: Emerges from agent-arena coupling (RR insight)

### Intelligence vs Wisdom
- **Intelligence**: Information processing capability (AI can achieve)
- **Rationality**: Overcoming self-deception (AI may approximate)
- **Wisdom**: Caring for reality beyond self (AI cannot achieve)
- **Consciousness**: Phenomenal experience (AI cannot achieve)

### Oracle Structure Standards

Every oracle follows this structure:
- `source-documents/` - Original sources with `NN-` numbered prefixes
- `{topic-folders}/` - Knowledge organized by topic with `00-`, `01-`, `02-` prefixes
- `INDEX.md` - Master file listing (update when structure changes)
- `SKILL.md` - Oracle identity (update when major reorganization)

### File Naming Rules

- **Numbered prefixes required**: `00-overview.md`, `01-concept.md`, `02-advanced.md`
- **Lowercase with hyphens**: `quantum-entanglement.md` not `Quantum_Entanglement.md`
