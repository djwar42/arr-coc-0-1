## Core Philosophy Summary

### The Creative Act Key Ideas:

1. **Everyone is a creator** - Creativity is fundamental to being human
2. **Serve the work** - Your ego must step aside
3. **Beginner's mind** - Approach each project fresh
4. **Four phases** - Seed ‚Üí Experimentation ‚Üí Craft ‚Üí Completion
5. **Awareness** - Pay attention to everything
6. **Trust the process** - Have faith even in uncertainty

### Production Philosophy Key Ideas:

1. **Strip to essence** - Remove everything unnecessary
2. **The best song wins** - Not the best production
3. **Create safe space** - Artists need freedom to explore
4. **Be honest** - Direct feedback with kindness
5. **Be patient** - Great work can't be rushed
6. **Genre is irrelevant** - Principles are universal
