## Key Research Findings

### IMRaD Structure (2024)
From ResearchPal Blog and University of Minnesota guides:
- Universal format for scientific papers
- Ensures reproducibility and clarity
- Aligns with peer review expectations
- Facilitates rapid information location

### Abstract Writing Principles (Sims, 2024)
From The Clinical Teacher:
- Use Aristotelian rhetoric (ethos, pathos, logos)
- Apply "problem-gap-hook" heuristic
- Think "story, not study" (Lingard & Watling)
- Edit ruthlessly—every word counts
- Include specific results, not vague summaries

### Methods Section Clarity (2024)
From Editverse and academic journals:
- Should be 2-3 pages or ~750 words
- Must enable complete replication
- Include design, participants, procedures, analysis
- Demonstrate rigor through transparency
