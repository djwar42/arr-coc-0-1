# Download Time Estimates & Optimization

## 1. Time Estimates by Bandwidth
- **100 Mbps:** ~10 days
- **500 Mbps:** ~2 days
- **1 Gbps:** ~1 day
- **10 Gbps:** ~3 hours

## 2. Optimization Strategies
1. Parallel downloads (4-8 processes)
2. Resumable downloads (aria2c/wget -c)
3. Off-peak hours (less congestion)
4. CDN proximity (Meta's global CDN)

Download 10 tars first (~100GB) for quick training start.
