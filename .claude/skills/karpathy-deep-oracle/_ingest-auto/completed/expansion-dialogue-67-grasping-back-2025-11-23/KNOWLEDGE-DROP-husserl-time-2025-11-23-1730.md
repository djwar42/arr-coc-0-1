## Key Concepts Covered

### Core Structure
- **Retention**: Passive, immediate, non-objectifying awareness of just-elapsed phase
- **Primal Impression**: Consciousness of strict now-phase
- **Protention**: Anticipatory consciousness of about-to-come phase

### Critical Distinctions
- Retention vs Memory (7 key differences documented)
- Living present as "standing-streaming" flow
- Temporal width vs punctual time
- Non-temporal temporalizing (avoiding infinite regress)

### Double Intentionality
- Longitudinal (Langsintentionalitat) - self-awareness along flow
- Transverse (Querintentionalitat) - object-awareness across succession

### Three Levels of Time
1. Internal time-consciousness (absolute constituting flow)
2. Subjective/personalistic time (experienced duration)
3. Objective/world time (clock time, measurement)
