## Key Insights

### SOAR Strengths
- Natural emergence of planning through subgoaling
- Proven scalability (TacAir-Soar, StarCraft AI)
- Chunking enables automatic skill acquisition
- Universal weak method property

### ACT-R Strengths
- Excellent cognitive modeling (predicts human performance)
- ~50ms cycle time validated empirically
- Subsymbolic mechanisms match neural data
- Extensive psychological validation

### CLARION Strengths
- Captures explicit-implicit distinction
- Bottom-up skill learning
- Neurally-inspired dual processing
- Flexible environmental adaptation

### ARR-COC-VIS Synthesis
**From SOAR**: Problem space framing, hierarchical processing
**From ACT-R**: Modular organization, activation-based mechanisms
**From CLARION**: Dual processing, bottom-up learning
**Unique**: Vervaekean opponent processing, transjective relevance
