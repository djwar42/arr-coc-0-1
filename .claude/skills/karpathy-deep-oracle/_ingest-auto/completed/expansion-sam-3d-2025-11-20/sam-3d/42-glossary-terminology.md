# Glossary & Terminology

**Key terms and acronyms in SAM 3D and 3D reconstruction**

---

## A-F

**AMASS:** Archive of Motion Capture as Surface Shapes. Mocap dataset.

**Axis-Angle:** 3D rotation representation (axis direction + angle magnitude).

**Blendshape:** Linear combination of mesh deformations for facial animation.

**Chamfer Distance:** Mesh quality metric (point-to-mesh distance).

**COCO:** Common Objects in Context. 2D keypoint dataset.

**Depth Ambiguity:** Multiple 3D interpretations from single 2D image.

**FACS:** Facial Action Coding System. Psychology-based facial expressions.

**FP16:** 16-bit floating point (half precision). 2× faster than FP32.

---

## G-M

**Ground Truth (GT):** True 3D annotations (for evaluation).

**HMR:** Human Mesh Recovery. 2D image → 3D human mesh.

**INT8:** 8-bit integer quantization. 4× faster than FP32.

**IoU:** Intersection over Union. Mask overlap metric.

**Kinematic Tree:** Skeletal hierarchy (parent-child joints).

**Linear Blend Skinning (LBS):** Mesh deformation via bone weights.

**LOD:** Level of Detail. Adaptive mesh complexity.

**Manifold:** Valid mesh topology (no self-intersections).

**MANO:** Hand parametric model (21 joints).

**MPJPE:** Mean Per-Joint Position Error. Standard HMR metric.

**MViT:** Multiscale Vision Transformer.

---

## N-S

**NeRF:** Neural Radiance Fields. Multi-view 3D reconstruction.

**Objaverse:** 800K+ 3D model repository.

**Occlusion:** Body parts hidden from camera view.

**PA-MPJPE:** Procrustes-Aligned MPJPE. Scale/rotation invariant metric.

**Penetration:** Mesh self-intersection (artifact).

**Pose Prior:** Learned distribution over body poses.

**Pseudo-GT:** Approximate ground truth (fitted SMPL).

**Reprojection Error:** 3D→2D consistency check.

**RLE:** Run-Length Encoding. Compact mask format.

**SMPL:** Skinned Multi-Person Linear model. Parametric body (69 params).

**SMPL-X:** Extended SMPL (hands + face).

**SMPL-H:** SMPL with hands (111 joints).

---

## T-Z

**TensorRT:** NVIDIA optimization library (1.5-2× speedup).

**Temporal Consistency:** Smooth pose transitions across video frames.

**Triangulation:** 3D point from multi-view 2D observations.

**Visibility:** Binary mask (which joints are visible).

**Watertight:** Mesh with no holes (closed surface).

**Zero-Shot:** Generalize to unseen categories (no fine-tuning).

---

## SAM 3D Specific

**SA-3DAO:** SAM 3D evaluation dataset (3D objects).

**SAM 3D Objects:** Single-image object 3D reconstruction.

**SAM 3D Body:** Single-image human mesh recovery.

**Diffusion Shortcuts:** Fast 3D generation via diffusion models.

---

## ARR-COC Terms

**Propositional Knowing:** Facts and spatial relationships.

**Perspectival Knowing:** Attention and relevance focus.

**Participatory Knowing:** Affordances and actions.

**Relevance Realization:** Dynamically allocating attention to salient information.

**Spatial Grounding:** Anchoring language understanding in 3D geometry.
