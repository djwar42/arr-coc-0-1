# Future Directions & Open Challenges

**Research frontiers for 3D human reconstruction and SAM 3D evolution**

---

## 1. Open Challenges

**4D Reconstruction:**
- Current: 3D per-frame
- Future: Consistent 4D (space + time)
- Challenge: Temporal surface tracking

**Dynamic Clothing:**
- Current: Static mesh
- Future: Physics-based cloth simulation
- Challenge: Real-time cloth dynamics

**Multi-Person Interaction:**
- Current: Independent per-person
- Future: Model physical contact (hugs, handshakes)
- Challenge: Interaction reasoning

---

## 2. Scaling to Extreme Scenarios

**Dense Crowds (50+ people):**
- Current: 10-20 people max
- Future: Concert crowds, protests
- Challenge: Occlusion, computation

**Extreme Occlusion (>90%):**
- Current: Hallucinate occluded parts
- Future: Multi-view fusion, temporal context
- Challenge: Uncertainty quantification

---

## 3. Emerging Techniques

**Neural Radiance Fields (NeRF):**
- Integrate NeRF for detailed geometry
- SAM 3D: Fast initialization for NeRF

**Diffusion Models:**
- Generative 3D models
- SAM 3D + diffusion: High-quality + fast

**Foundation Models:**
- Unified model for all 3D tasks
- SAM 3D is step toward 3D foundation model
