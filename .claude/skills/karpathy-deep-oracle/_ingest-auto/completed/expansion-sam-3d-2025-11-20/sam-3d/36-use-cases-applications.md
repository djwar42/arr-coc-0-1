# Use Cases & Applications

**Real-world applications of SAM 3D across industries**

---

## 1. VR/AR & Metaverse

**Full-Body Avatars:**
- Selfie → 3D avatar (5 seconds)
- Personalized VR representation
- Use: Social VR (Meta Horizon, VRChat)

**AR Furniture:**
- Photo of furniture → 3D model
- Place virtually in room (IKEA app)
- Try before you buy

---

## 2. Content Creation

**3D Asset Generation:**
- Concept art → 3D game asset
- 100× faster than manual modeling
- Use: Game development, film VFX

**Motion Capture:**
- Video → 3D human animations
- No mocap suit required
- Use: Animation studios, indie games

---

## 3. E-Commerce

**Product Visualization:**
- Product photo → Interactive 3D model
- 360° viewer on website
- Result: 30% higher conversion rates

**Virtual Try-On:**
- Clothing photo → 3D garment mesh
- Try on user's avatar
- Use: Fashion e-commerce

---

## 4. Healthcare

**Physical Therapy:**
- Patient video → 3D pose analysis
- Track joint angles, range of motion
- Quantitative progress metrics

**Gait Analysis:**
- Walking video → 3D gait parameters
- Diagnose movement disorders
- Use: Orthopedics, neurology

---

## 5. Sports & Fitness

**Biomechanics:**
- Athlete video → 3D pose analysis
- Optimize technique (golf swing, pitching)
- Result: 5-10% performance gain

**Form Checking:**
- Workout video → Exercise form analysis
- Prevent injuries (squat depth, deadlift back angle)
- Use: Fitness apps (Peloton, Mirror)
